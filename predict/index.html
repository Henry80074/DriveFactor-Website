Predict

Predictive risk intelligence for roads and regions

View risk maps

High resolution risk maps for every requirement.

DriveFactor Predict delivers segment, junction and corridor-level risk intelligence so teams can act before crashes accumulate. Our predictive AI learns from network geometry, exposure, and temporal patterns, enabling accurate forecasts - even on new roads with limited crash history.

Baseline road risk

  • Segment-level predictions — high resolution risk at each link and junction approach.
  • Corridor summaries — aggregated views to prioritise interventions across longer stretches.
  • Regional Forecasts — Predicts crash probabilities and risk metrics to help plan operations and enforcement.

Dynamic road risk

  • Weather — Models that adjust risk scores based on current weather conditions, enabling real-time risk assessment.
  • Sunglare — Understand when and where sunglare has the greatest impact on visibility.
  • Temporal — Hour/ day/ seasonal features for understanding how risk changes throughout the year.
