Create safer drivers using the power of contextual insights

Typical telematics data has a problem. It lacks the context required to provide meaningful insights and personalised training. Coach turns noisy telematics data into trusted, contextual intelligence by enriching every event with road geometry, time‑of‑day and weather conditions

  1. Accurate performance analysis: Consistent scoring for speed, braking, junction handling, night driving and weather sensitivity.
  2. Targeted coaching: Evidence‑linked recommendations that show drivers exactly what to improve and why.
  3. Contextual intelligence: Enriched event data that provides the context needed for fair, evidence‑backed scores that explain behaviour, prioritise risk and support driver coaching.
  4. Driver buy‑in: Fairer feedback, clear progress, and practical coaching that improves engagement and reduces incidents.
  5. Defensible outcomes: Transparent audit trails for compliance, insurer reviews and claims reduction.

The result: fewer incidents, lower claims, and a coaching programme drivers will actually buy into.

How Coach turns data into action

A clear, repeatable process that makes coaching consistent, defensible, and measurable.

  1. Ingest: Trip GPS, events, and telematics streams.
  2. Contextualise: Map‑matching, weather conditions, exposure, time‑of‑day.
  3. Score & explain: Behaviour models with per‑event evidence.
  4. Coach: Targeted interventions, alerts and training assignments.
