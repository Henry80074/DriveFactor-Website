Products

Six geospatial analytics products built on the DriveFactor Road Risk Engine. Each addresses a distinct question in road safety and network risk management.

🗺️
Layer 1 · Spatial

Spatial Risk Maps

Per-segment crash probability derived from road geometry, junction type, speed limit and historical crash density. The baseline risk surface for any network.

View product →
📡
Layer 3 · Live traffic

Dynamic Traffic Risk Maps

Traffic speed deviation from per-link baselines converted to crash odds ratios via a validated varying-coefficient GAM. Covers Aimsun-modelled and live WebTRIS data.

View product →
☀️
Environmental

Sun Glare Intensity Maps

Road bearing compared to solar azimuth at each time window. Segments within ±5° of direct solar angle flagged as severe glare exposure — historically 2–4× crash risk uplift.

View product →
🌧️
Environmental

Rain & Low Light Risk

Combined precipitation and darkness risk layer derived from L2 temporal-weather model outputs. Identifies which corridors are most vulnerable to combined adverse conditions.

View product →
⚙️
Analysis tool

What-If Scenario Planner

Interactive dose-response tool. Adjust hypothetical speed, weather, time and road conditions to immediately see how L2 × L3 combined risk changes across the network. No re-simulation required.

View product →
🔔
Live data

Live Risk Alerts

15-minute L3 risk scores computed from WebTRIS inductive loop sensor data. Configurable threshold alerting for PM peak corridors. Integrates with Aimsun and Confluence.

View product →
