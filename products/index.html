Products

From baseline risk mapping to intervention planning, we build tools to help make roads safer.

Spatial risk

Spatial Risk Maps

Collision probability per road segment, predicted from road geometry, network layout, features and historical collisions. The static baseline layer of the DriveFactor risk engine.

View product →
Dynamic risk

Dynamic Risk Modelling

Models how traffic volume, weather, light conditions and time of day combine to shift collision likelihood across the network — giving a time-sensitive picture of where and when risk is elevated.

View product →
Environmental

Sun Glare Intensity Maps

Models sun trajectory alignment with road geometry to identify which segments face direct sun exposure by time of day and season, flagging periods of severe glare risk.

View product →
Exposure

Absolute Road Risk

Models collision risk per vehicle kilometre travelled, showing the likelihood that any given journey on a segment results in a collision. Exposes genuinely dangerous roads.

View product →
Analysis tool

Traffic Scenario Planner

Simulate how changes in traffic flow and congestion affect collision risk. Evaluate how traffic conditions drive risk across the road network to improve mitigation strategies.

View product →
Intervention planning

Road Safety Intervention Planner

Predict the expected collision reduction from a road safety intervention using measured scheme outcomes from comparable road segments across the UK network. Demonstrated here for 30 to 20 mph speed limit changes.
