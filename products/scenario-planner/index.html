How it works

The Traffic Scenario Planner draws on data from sensor networks to model the relationship between traffic flow conditions and collision likelihood across each road segment. Rather than treating risk as static, it captures how congestion, speed deviation and unexpected changes in traffic patterns each contribute to elevated risk on specific corridors.

Using the planner, you can simulate a range of traffic conditions and evaluate how risk responds across the network. For example, quantifying the safety impact of a diversion onto roads outside their normal flow range, or identifying where risk concentrates during a congested peak period.

Each segment is scored against its own baseline, so the planner identifies where conditions are creating anomalous risk rather than simply flagging busy roads. This gives road safety teams and transport planners a reliable basis for prioritising monitoring and making the case for targeted traffic management interventions.
