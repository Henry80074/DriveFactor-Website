Methodology

A multi-layer risk architecture

Road risk is shaped by many interacting factors. DriveFactor brings these together to create a comprehensive risk profile, enabling you to anticipate and understand road risk better than ever before.

L1

Road environment & collision history

Structural risk grounded in road geometry, junction type, speed limit, carriageway layout and over a decade of Stats19 recorded collision data.

Road geometry · Stats19 · OSM
L2

Temporal & environmental conditions

Risk varies significantly by time of day, day of week, season and weather. Fatigue-related patterns, darkness, precipitation, wind and other conditions known to influence collision likelihood are all considered.

Hour · Season · Weather · Darkness
L3

Traffic volume & speed behaviour

Measured and modelled traffic conditions, including flow estimates, speeds and driving behaviour, converting static risk into a dynamic scoring system based on real changes to traffic flow

Traffic Volume · Speed · Flow estimates
